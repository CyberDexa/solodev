## Emergency Protocols

### When Stuck (> 30 min on same problem)
1. **AI assistance**: Paste error + code, ask for help
2. **Simplify approach**: Maybe there's an easier way?
3. **Skip for now**: Mark as TODO, move to next task
4. **Research break**: 15 min maximum googling
5. **Ask community**: Stack Overflow, Reddit, Discord

### When Behind Schedule
1. **Scope review**: What can be cut from MVP?
2. **AI acceleration**: Generate more code, write less manually
3. **Template switching**: Move from Full App to MVP template
4. **Deadline adjustment**: Better to ship late than never
5. **Learning capture**: What caused the delay?

### When Losing Motivation
1. **Deploy something**: Even if incomplete, get it live
2. **Show progress**: Share screenshots, demos
3. **Review past wins**: Check completed projects
4. **Take a break**: Sometimes 24h rest helps
5. **Reminder of purpose**: Why are you building this?
